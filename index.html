I may be a beginner myself, but I can at least get you on the right track.

Years back in 2014, I played bass with my brother in our band. I wasn’t great, but it was then that I realized how fun music can be. Unfortunately, I didn’t stick with it all that time, and back in december of 2026 I decided to give it another go

Back in 2014 I had my brother at hand ready to give me pointers, but not anymore, I had to learn on my own through trial and error. Hopefully, I can impart onto you what I have learned to save you the hassle of figuring out where to even begin.

I will get into gear recommendations to get you started, no matter if you want to tackle acoustic, bass, or electric guitar, just the bare minimum materials needed for entry, some additional accessory recommendations if you want them, some small tips by me and other resources to take your learning to the next level

I will aim to find gear and resources that save you as much money as possible while getting you on the right track. Learning music should be accessible to all people of all economic backgrounds. I will list free and paid learning resources with clear indication on how much you can expect to pay.
